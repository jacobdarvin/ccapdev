F.A.Q.

What is Catvas?

Canvas is a 2nd year student project created by Jacob Darvin, Justin Galura and Michelle Gelvoleo, its goal is to establish itself in the DLSU PUSA community as a social network surrounding them.

Where do my donations go?

Your donations go directly to maintaining the servers of catvas and DLSU PUSA funds.

How can I help?

You can help by sharing catvas, to your peers and friends.
